What FinCA analyses
Real expenses
Calculates your actual investable surplus after rent, EMIs, groceries, dependents, and lifestyle. No flat 20% assumption.
Monte Carlo engine
Runs 800 market simulations showing worst, likely, and best case retirement outcomes based on real Nifty 50 volatility data.
Income growth model
Shows how your corpus grows as your salary increases over time — not just a static SIP assumption for the next 35 years.
Loan detection
Flags high-interest loans above 12% and removes that EMI burden before calculating your investable amount.
Education fund
Projects future education costs for child dependents using historical CPI inflation data from the Reserve Bank of India.
Tax optimisation
Detects your tax bracket and shows exactly how much you can save through ELSS investments under Section 80C.
